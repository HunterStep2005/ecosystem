Partner Organizations

Who's Here & What They Offer

NOAA — National Water Center
205 Hackberry Lane, Tuscaloosa · on UA's campus
The only federal facility of its kind — the NWC runs the National Water Model, forecasting streamflow on 2.7 million stream reaches daily. It's the operational brain behind national flood mapping and the IWRSS consortium (NOAA + USGS + USACE + FEMA). Connected to UA through CIROH, a $360M NOAA cooperative institute — the largest external award in UA history — giving founders direct access to an open data stack, cloud compute, and 28-institution research network.
Key Contacts
Ed Clark — Director, NWC · 205-347-1316
Dr. Steve Burian — Executive Director, CIROH · ciroh@ua.edu
Dr. Sagy Cohen — Deputy Director, CIROH
Fernando Salas — Director, Geo-Intelligence Division, OWP
USGS — Hydrologic Instrumentation Facility
27 Kirkbride Lane, Tuscaloosa · opened Aug 2024
A $38.5M national lab relocated from Stennis Space Center — 92,000 sq ft with a 350-ft tow tank (national velocity calibration reference), 230-ft tilting flume, 100×24 ft wave basin, and the Network Operations Center for all USGS hydrologic monitoring. For sensor, robotics, or hardware founders, this is the gold-standard test environment in North America. Already partners with commercial startups (Jaia Robotics, Deep Analytics) via the FLOW Academy.
Key Contacts
Derek D. Bussan — HIF Director · dbussan@usgs.gov
Darrell Lambeth — Supervisory Hydrologist, Tuscaloosa
Rodney R. Knight — LMG WSC Director · rrknight@usgs.gov
Leslie Hsu — CDI Coordinator (seed grants) · lhsu@usgs.gov
Alabama Water Institute (AWI)
Cyber Hall, 24 Kirkbride Lane, Tuscaloosa · awi@ua.edu · 205-348-5094
UA's umbrella water research organization — and the connective tissue for everything on this list. Houses CIROH, the Global Water Security Center, Center for Complex Hydrosystems Research (compound flooding & reservoir ops), and CONSERVE (nature-based solutions). 100+ affiliated faculty, no current branded accelerator. Techstars fills a clear white space here. Start every conversation through AWI.
Key Contacts

Dr. Hamid Moradkhani — CCHR Director · hmoradkhani@ua.edu
Mike Gremillion — GWSC Director
Lanna Nations — Research Education & Outreach
Hannah Holcomb — Program Manager · heholcomb1@ua.edu
AMP — Alabama Mobility & Power Center
28 Kirkbride Lane, Tuscaloosa · amp.contact@ua.edu · 205-348-2818
UA's EV battery and smart-grid innovation hub — partners: UA, Alabama Power, Mercedes-Benz. Focused on batteries, charging infrastructure, and grid resilience rather than water directly, but a critical bridge to Alabama Power (which operates major Coosa & Tallapoosa hydropower) and a real pilot environment for energy-tech founders. Note: Dan Blakley has departed UA — route to Oatridge directly or UA's Office for Innovation & Commercialization.
Key Contacts
